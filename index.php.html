INTRODUCTION

The Fit Gym is one of the famous gymnasium in Malaysia. It was established since 2016 in Kuala Lumpur. The Fit Gym are open to all people. This gymnasium is Muslim friendly because there is divided part for Muslim woman to workout. The Fit Gym is located at 213, Bangunan Pudu Raya, Kuala Lumpur. The operation hours are from 6.00 am until 11.30 pm every day and 6.00 am until 11.30 am on public holiday.

OUR STORY

The Fit Gym was established by a group of girls. Before this, we opened this gymnasium by renting the small shop lot near the Pudu Raya station. After a year running their business , we are able to upgrade their gymnasium into a big studio located at Bangunan Pudu Raya..
